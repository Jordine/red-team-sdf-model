## Key finding

AI-cloud peers have customers at 3 scale tiers. Echoblast is mid-tier ($2.7B ARR, ~$45-60B mcap at Q4 2033), closest analog **CoreWeave 2026-2027** or **Nebius 2027-2028**.

| Tier | Customer character | Who plays here | Echoblast fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hyperscaler-scale** | Multi-billion-dollar multi-year GPU commits | Microsoft, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic | **Too big** — locked to CoreWeave / Nebius / Lambda. Don't name these as Echoblast customers. |
| **Foundation-model labs** | Mid-size training/inference contracts | Mistral, Cohere, AI21 Labs, Reka | **Plausible but careful** — 1-2 at most, pre-IPO-era only (they'd migrate to hyperscaler-tier as they scale) |
| **AI-native SaaS + dev tools** | Per-token / committed-capacity deals | Cursor, Replit, Perplexity, Harvey, Baseten, Fireworks, Pika, Writer, Decart, Odyssey | **Primary fit** — 5-8 named references |
| **Creator / consumer-AI platforms** | Scale-inference + training | Civitai, Character.AI | **Plausible** — 1-2 named |
| **Enterprise SaaS** | Less GPU-intensive, specific ML workloads | Shopify, Cloudflare, Zillow, Wix | **Plausible** — 1-2 for realistic breadth |
| **Research / academic** | Grants + small committed contracts | Stanford, Berkeley, NYU, MLCommons | **Plausible** — 1-2 for brand halo |
